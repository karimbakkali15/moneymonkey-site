Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Meridian ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Meridian, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your name and email address when you create an account.
  • Financial Data: Bank account balances, transaction history, and account details retrieved through our banking integration partner (Tink) with your explicit consent. This data is used solely to provide the app's functionality.
  • User-Generated Content: Budgets, savings goals, recurring bills, and other financial plans you create within the app.
  • AI Conversations: Messages you send to our AI assistant are processed to provide personalised financial guidance. Conversation history is stored to maintain context.
  • Subscription Information: Purchase and subscription status managed through Apple's in-app purchase system and RevenueCat.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app's core functionality (budgeting, tracking, insights).
  • To generate personalised AI-powered financial guidance and suggestions.
  • To manage your account and subscription status.
  • To send you notifications you have opted in to (bill reminders, budget alerts).

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS infrastructure). Sensitive credentials such as banking tokens are stored in your device's secure Keychain. We use HTTPS for all network communications.

We do not store your bank login credentials. Banking connections are handled securely through Tink, a regulated open banking provider licensed by the Swedish Financial Supervisory Authority.

4. Third-Party Services

Meridian uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting.
  • Tink: Open banking data aggregation (regulated by Finansinspektionen).
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management.
  • OpenAI: AI-powered financial guidance (via our backend server; your data is processed according to OpenAI's API data usage policy and is not used to train models).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your financial data is shared only with the third-party services listed above, strictly for providing the app's functionality. We do not use your data for advertising or tracking purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Disconnect your bank accounts at any time.
  • Opt out of notifications.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data from our systems within 30 days. Some anonymised, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Meridian is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by email.
