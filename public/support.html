Frequently asked questions

How do I connect my bank account?

During onboarding or in Settings, tap "Connect Bank". You'll be redirected to Tink's secure banking portal where you can safely log in to your bank. Meridian never sees your bank login credentials.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone Settings > tap your name > Subscriptions > Meridian > Cancel Subscription. Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app > scroll to the bottom > tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. Bank connections are handled through Tink, a regulated open banking provider. Sensitive tokens are stored in your device's secure Keychain, and all network communication uses HTTPS encryption.

Why aren't my transactions showing?

Try pulling down to refresh on the Home screen. If transactions still don't appear, go to Settings > Bank Connection and reconnect your bank. Some banks may take a few minutes to sync.

Can I restore my purchase on a new device?

Yes. Go to Settings > Subscription > Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

Is the AI financial guidance accurate?

Meridian's AI provides general insights based on your spending patterns. It is not a substitute for professional financial advice. Always consult a qualified advisor for major financial decisions.
